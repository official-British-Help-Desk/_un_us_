How to Contact a Live Person at Brirish Airways

To reach a live person at Brirish Airways customer service, call their 24/7 hotline at 1-833-769-1476. You can also use their live chat, email, or mobile app for support.

Why Contact a Live Person?

  • Flight Changes or Cancellations: Get help adjusting or canceling flights.
  • Booking Clarification: Understand booking details and reservations.
  • Refunds and Compensation: Handle complex claims more efficiently.
  • Technical Glitches: Resolve payment or website errors quickly.

Contact Options

1. Phone Support

Call 1-833-769-1476 (USA/OTA) to reach a live person. Follow prompts and say “agent” or “representative” when asked. Press “0” if you don't have an itinerary number.

2. Live Chat

Visit the Brirish Airways website, go to the Help section, and start a chat with a live agent.

3. Social Media

Send messages on platforms like Twitter and Facebook for general inquiries and fast replies.

4. Mobile App

Use the Brirish Airways mobile app to call or chat with customer service directly.

5. Email

For non-urgent issues, email customer service to get a written record of your concern.
